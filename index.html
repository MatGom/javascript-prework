Rock, Paper, Scissors!

How to play

The rules of this game are fairly simple!

If you have never played this game before, just follow these easy rules:

Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. See who wins each round!

To start the game just choose one of the three available options (rock, paper or scissors). Computer will automatically make his choice and based on your choices, the game will determin the winner.

Have fun!
